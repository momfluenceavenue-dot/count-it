Income by Stream

See Where Your
Money Is Coming From.

This breakdown updates automatically as you log income. Use it to see which income streams are working and where to focus your energy. Below the breakdown is everything you need to know to maximize each stream.

📦
Digital Products
The highest leverage income stream. Create once, sell forever. Your product is always working even when you are not.
Price between $17–$97 for best conversion
Sell in at least 3 videos per week minimum
Use Gumroad or Stan Store for instant delivery
Create a bundle to increase average order value
🔗
Affiliate Marketing
Promote products you already use and love. Your authenticity is your conversion rate. Never promote something you have not tried.
Amazon Associates pays 1–10% per sale
LTK and RewardStyle work well for lifestyle creators
Always disclose affiliate relationships — it builds trust
Include affiliate links in every relevant video caption
🎁
TikTok Live Gifts
The most underused income stream by new creators. A loyal audience of even 50 people showing up to your live can generate real income.
Go live minimum 3x per week, 30+ minutes each
Always have a topic — teach something real every live
Mention your products at least 3 times per live
Thank gifters by name — it encourages more gifts
🤝
Brand Deals
Brands pay to access your audience. Even small but highly engaged audiences are valuable. Your niche clarity is your pitch.
Build a one-page media kit with your stats and niche
Pitch brands in your niche directly via email or DM
Start with gifted deals to build your portfolio
Charge based on engagement rate not just follower count
💼
Coaching & Services
Your knowledge and time have enormous value. A single coaching client can pay more than 50 digital product sales.
Start with a $97 discovery call — low barrier, high value
Position coaching as the next step after your digital products
Use Calendly or a booking link in your bio
Limit spots to create urgency and exclusivity
💰
Creator Fund
Passive income from TikTok based on your views. Not the highest payer but every dollar adds up when stacked with your other streams.
Must have 10K+ followers and 100K views in 30 days to qualify
Typical payout: $0.02–$0.04 per 1,000 views
Do not build your strategy around this — treat it as a bonus
TikTok Creativity Program pays significantly more — check eligibility
